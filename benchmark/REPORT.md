## 5. Coverage & honesty notes

- **CPT** is the statistically meaningful part (n=51 FeCl₃-basis, leave-one-out).
- **CO₂ is a small, indicative spot-check (n=10 in-scope), not a definitive validation.** Two cited cases are deliberately shown as *out of model scope*: a 13Cr CRA (passivates) and a top-of-line condensing case (not a bulk-flowline regime). A bulk carbon-steel model getting these "wrong" is correct behaviour.
- **Growth path (human-gated):** expanding to 150–300 cited measured cases across CO₂/CPT/sour is the planned next step (see `PLAN-differentiation.md`, move #1). New cases must be cited and traceable; none are to be invented.
